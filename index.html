HISTORY

The presence of out-of-school children due to economic inability, causes the majority of them to wander the streets. Very poor living conditions leading to malnutrition, lack of hygiene, low morale, extreme poverty, and factors that contribute to the children being exposed to violence and sexual abuse are all factors that pushed SMAC Sisters to gather these children and give them a home.



MISSION

SMAC's mission is to seek out children left on the streets, abandoned and orphaned, provide them with opportunities to improve their lives through education and provide them with a home where they can feel love and care.







PROGRAMS

SMAC sources its sponsors from both domestic and foreign organizations and people. Unfortunately, because of the pandemic's rapid spread, the sponsorship has declined. SMAC gives the children practical skills in the form of cooking, baking, and other livelihood tasks. They also gave the kids access to a covered court where they could play and participate in programs and activities.
